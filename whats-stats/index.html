WhatsStats lets you upload your exported WhatsApp chat and instantly explore fun, interactive stats about your conversations.
All processing happens in your browser.
Your data is never uploaded or stored.
How to export your chat ▼
  1. Open the chat → tap ⋮ → More → Export Chat.
  2. Choose Without Media — WhatsApp saves a .zip (or you can share the .txt).
  3. Upload that .zip or .txt below.
  1. Open the chat → tap the contact or group name at the top.
  2. Tap Export Chat → Without Media — you get a .zip (or the .txt).
  3. Upload that .zip or .txt below.
Note: WhatsApp exports are limited to ~40,000 messages per file
or

Tap Upload your chat to choose a file, or drag & drop a .txt or .zip (exported chat).
